Apply on Hindus first: Opposition slams PM Modi’s Uniform Civil Code pitch

Hours after Prime Minister Narendra Modi batted for the Uniform Civil Code (UCC) pitch whiles addressing BJP workers in poll-bound Madhya Pradesh, Opposition parties slammed him for his remarks and called it an attack on “India’s pluralism and diversity”.By India Today News Desk: Hours after Prime Minister Narendra Modi made a pitch for the Uniform Civil Code (UCC) and lashed out at the opposition for playing "vote bank politics"

Attacking the Opposition while addressing BJP workers under the ‘Mera Booth Sabse Majboot’ campaign in poll-bound Madhya Pradesh on Tuesday, PM Modi said the people are being instigated in the name of the Uniform Civil Code (UCC). He also asked how the country can function with dual laws that govern personal matters, and accused the Opposition of using the UCC issue to "mislead and provoke" the Muslim community.

PM Modi said, “Will a family function if there are two different sets of rules for people? Then how will a country run? Our Constitution too guarantees equal rights to all people.”"India's PM considers India's diversity and its pluralism a problem. So, he says such things...Will you strip the country of its pluralism and diversity in the name of a UCC? When he speaks of UCC, he is speaking of the Hindu Civil Code...I challenge him .

Enforcement Directorate summons Saayoni Ghosh in teachers' recruitment scam case

Trinamool Congress youth state president and Bengali actress Saayoni Ghosh will on Friday appear before the ED, in connection with the teachers' recruitment scam case in West Bengal. The Enforcement Directorate (ED) on Tuesday summoned Trinamool Congress youth state president and Bengali actress Saayoni Ghosh, in connection with the teachers' recruitment scam case in West Bengal. At the beginning of June this year, the West Bengal government had approached the Supreme Court challenging the Calcutta High Court order.

Ghosh was asked to appear before the investigating agency in Kolkata on Friday. According to sources, the Bengali actress would be grilled over some suspicious financial transactions and arrested and expelled TMC youth leader, Kuntal Ghosh, who was arrested in an education scam case by the probe agency in January this year. The West Bengal government, through Advocate Sunil Fernandes, mentioned the case before the Supreme Court vacation bench of Justices Sudhanshu Dhulia
